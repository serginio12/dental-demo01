Where Are We?

The practice is easily accessible where the A40 meets the A487, on West Street in Fishguard, Pembrokeshire, and is conveniently situated a few minutes’ walk from the bus stop on Fishguard square. It has limited parking time (up to 1 hour on our doorstep), as well as a place for disabled parking (up to 2 hours). There is also a large car park available around the corner in the West Street car park (sat nav parking SA65 9AR). For those who travel by train from other parts of the county a regular shuttle-bus service operates between ‘Fishguard and Goodwick’ train station, and West Street in Fishguard.
