REFERRALS

West Street Practice Plan is welcoming patient referral from West Wales. We are specialised in the dental treatment of nervous patients. If you think that your patients are displaying signs of distress or anxiety whilst undergoing routine dental treatment, please let us know, so we may inform them of our sedation techniques.

For E-Referrals the following information is required:

  • Complete patient details and up to date contact numbers;
  • Relevant X-rays and full medical history
  • Dental charting and a plan of required treatment;
  • Patient referral form

When requesting a treatment for one of your patients, please ensure that you complete the E-Referral form or download the PDF format HERE to enable us to carry out the dental treatment required.

If you need to speak with any of our support team or have any queries, telephone us on : 01348873370 or 07377376699 or email : weststreetdental@yahoo.co.uk

At the WSDP we look after your patients as we would expect to be looked after ourselves.
